By DJ Donna B.

    Songs Previously Requested

DJ Donna B. has always been a lover of music. She has been enjoying since she was a child. Growing up, her home has always been a musical family. Her dad played baritone and bass guitar. Her younger brother played baritone and trombone. Her youngest brother played trumpet, and her mom was the singer.

She also enjoyed all types of music. Her favorite genre is gospel, but she also liked R&B, classical, blues, soul, neosoul, jazz, pop and country. She started exploring with the world of music as a singer/songwriter/arranger in 6th grade, after learning to playing the clarinet. She was first chair for most of middle school. In high school, she was first chair parts of her junior and senior year. She was also one of 3 drum majors for the marching band her senior year.

She believes in the power of music as a healer. Music can uplift your day, motivate and empower individuals. She believes the power of music should not be taken lightly. So with this website, she shares her love of music by sharing this music box with you. Hope you enjoy.
