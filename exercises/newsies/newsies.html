Donna's Developer Journey

July 19, 2018 - Donna Bradshaw was just your typical adult working to make sure she was able to survive in life. Going through the day to day motions of a job.

Donna has decided ENOUGH IS ENOUGH. She has decided to go back to school to learn the mechanics of web development and software engineering. With the help of VSchool, Donna has embarked upon an exciting journey to the technology land that only a few can call home.

Over the course of the next 3 months, she is going to have a rigorous cirriculum, assignments and projects to plow through to get her ready for the next phase of her life. Donna looks forward to being a full-stack developer and eventually wants to end up as a Senior Software Engineer, where she can contribute to the world
